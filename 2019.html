Lionel Messi won his record-breaking sixth Ballon d’Or in 2019. He had a remarkable season with FC Barcelona, leading La Liga in both goals and assists, and continued to demonstrate unmatched consistency and creativity on the field.

This win reaffirmed Messi’s dominance and artistry, as he edged out Virgil van Dijk and Cristiano Ronaldo in one of the closest Ballon d’Or votes of the modern era.
